About Luckylettuce

These are the spices of our lives. Lettuce celebrate together. Jess Palmer created Luckylettuce out of a love of lettuce, salads with a kick and magical spices!

"Ever since I was a child, I would play in my grandmother's garden, help prepare family meals and really loved to experiment with spices. My grandma could make a potato taste 15 different ways and that fascinated me. Now it's my goal to provide people with a choice to really change up their meals, with only a pinch of creativity!"

Just a salad waiting for a spice.
